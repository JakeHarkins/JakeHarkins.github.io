BMW Project

This project is currently under NDA, and as such some details cannot be discussed. Limited further details may be discussed in private settings.

This project is focused on delivering a new game experience for BMW in partnership with the QUT Design Academy. Core aspects of the game deliver educational aspects for younger children (8-10 years old) and playing on Android Devices.

Contributions

In the development of this project, I acted as Lead Programmer, devoting development to a series of core systems present in the game, the associated companion and showcasing applications.

  • Non-Player Character Control
  • Player Control
  • Unity Networking + Asymmetric Gameplay
  • Companion + Showcasing Applications
