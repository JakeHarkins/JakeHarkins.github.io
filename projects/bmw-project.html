BMW Project

This project was completed during my Capstone (IGB300/IGB301) in my penultimate year of university, acting as a Programmer in Team Acuity, composed of myself, Mark Auman, Rebecca Jeffries and Nathan Prestidge. We developed a new mobile game experience for BMW in collaboration with the BMW/QUT Design Academy located on QUT's Kelvin Grove campus. Our core focus for the game was to deliver an educational game experience for younger children, aged 8-10 years old, which aligned with BMW's environmental and education goals.

Contributions

In the development of this project, I acted as Lead Programmer, devoting development to a series of core systems present in the game, alongside the associated companion and showcasing applications.

My primary focus was the Player interactions with the controlled non-Player character and surrounding environment during gameplay sections.

My other focuses included the asymmmetric networking for the associated companion app, allowing for family members to participate in the gameplay without having additional devices on hand.
