BMW Project

This project was completed during my Capstone (IGB300/IGB301) in my penultimate year of university, acting as a Programmer in Team Acuity, composed of myself, Mark Auman, Rebecca Jeffries and Nathan Prestidge. We collaboratively worked on a new mobile game experience for BMW in collaboration with the BMW/QUT Design Academy located on QUT's Kelvin Grove campus. Our core focus for the game was to deliver an educational game experience for younger children aged 8-10 years old.

Contributions

In the development of this project, I acted as Lead Programmer, devoting development to a series of core systems present in the game, the associated companion and showcasing applications.

  • Non-Player Character Control
  • Player Control
  • Unity Networking + Asymmetric Gameplay
  • Companion + Showcasing Applications
