BMW + QUT Design Academy Project

Overview

This project was created in collaboration with the BMW/QUT Design Academy located on QUT's Kelvin Grove campus. Our focus was to create an educational game experience...

Team

This project was developed by Team Acuity, a team consisting of 4 members, Jake Harkins (Programmer), Mark Auman (Designer/Programmer), Rebecca Jeffries (Artist) and Nathan Prestidge (Designer/Prop Artist).

Key Contributions

I acted as Lead Programmer for this project, developing or assisting with the implementaion of a variety of core gameplay features.

  • Player Input Management
  • RPC-based Networking
  • Player/Voice System Integration

Contributions

In the development of this project, I acted as Lead Programmer, devoting development to a series of core systems present in the game, assisting other developers with the implementation of certain features, code styling documentation, and the creation of flow diagrams to assist explanations of the core gameplay loop, or the interaction of certain game features/systems.

The core systems I focussed my development on was the implementaion of the Player controls, networking solutions and Player management to allow the implementation other gameplay features. My networking development involved the implementation of and asymmetric multiplayer solution, which allows for family members to participate in gameplay without a need for additional devices.
