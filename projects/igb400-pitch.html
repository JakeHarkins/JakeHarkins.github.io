Alerian Defenders - Project Pitch

Overview

Upon the final approach of Aleria, a number of critical systems have failed. Your communications are gone, work with your squad to repair your ship and return to the surface.

Project Aims

The aims of this project are to create...

  • Mobile Party Game
  • 4 Player Local Multiplayer
  • Accessible to all ages

Project Outcomes

An increasing number of studios are looking for development skills across muliple platforms and Multiplayer networking. This project aims to provide a solid foundation in these skills.
