Current Project

Alerian Defenders - Game Pitch

Alerian Defenders is a party-based mobile multiplayer game where Players must work together in order to save the Concord from crashing into the surface of Aleria. Everyone has their tasks, but your screens are on the fritz. Cut through the chaos, get everything repaired, and touch down on the surface! Developed solo.

Past Projects

QUT VRES Project 24/25

As part of QUT's VRES Program, I conducted a research project on the inclusion of an LLM in a modular VR tutorial system to improve Player perception of tutorials and reduce development time for future VR experiences. This research was produced over the summer break of 2024/2025 under the supervision of Dr. Selen Turkay and Joel Harmon.

BMW Design Academy Project

A game experience developed as apart of the QUT IGB300 + IGB301 Games Capstone Project in my penultimate year of my degree. This project is an educational experience aimed at younger audiences on Andriod Devices with an accompanying companion app. With its involvement with BMW, this project currently exists under NDA and specific details cannot be discussed. Developed with Team Acuity.
